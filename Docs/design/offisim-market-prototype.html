22Active company switch — installed-state re-derive (per-company) Same Market listing renders differently in two companies because installedListingIds ∪ installedPackageKeys is scoped per active company. Company A has the package installed → CTA + pip read "Installed"; Company B does not → CTA reads "Install". The arrow between the two panes is the active-company switch event.
Active company · Acme Studios installed
Active company · Boreal Labs not installed

Switch event: scope.active_company_id changes → MarketPage re-runs deriveInstalledKeys() against the new company's installations rows → all mkt-card + md-install states re-derive in one tick. Nothing else (search / filter / sort / pagination) is reset.
